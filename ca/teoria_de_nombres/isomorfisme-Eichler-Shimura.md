$$H^1(\Gamma,V_{k-2})\cong M_k(\Gamma)\oplus \overline{S_k(\Gamma)}$$

On:
- $V_{k-2}$ és l'espai dual dels polinomis de grau $\leq k-2$.
- $H^1(\Gamma,V)=\frac{\mbox{cocicles}}{\mbox{covores}}=\frac{\mathrm{Z}^1(\Gamma,V)}{\mathrm B^1(\Gamma,V)}$.
- **Cocicles: ** $Z^1(\Gamma,V)=\{\varphi:\Gamma\to V \mid \varphi(\gamma_1\gamma_2)=\varphi(\gamma_1)+\gamma_1\varphi(\gamma_2)\}$.
- **Covores:** $B^1(\Gamma,V)=\{\varphi_v:\Gamma\to V \mid v\in V\}$.
